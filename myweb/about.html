About

What is intangible cultural heritage

An intangible cultural heritage (ICH) is a practice, representation, expression, knowledge, or skill considered by UNESCO to be part of a place's cultural heritage. Buildings, historic places, monuments, and artifacts are cultural property. Intangible heritage consists of nonphysical intellectual wealth, such as folklore, customs, beliefs, traditions, knowledge, and language. Intangible cultural heritage is considered by member states of UNESCO in relation to the tangible World Heritage focusing on intangible aspects of culture.

2001, UNESCO made a survey among States and NGOs to try to agree on a definition, and the Convention for the Safeguarding of the Intangible Cultural Heritage was drafted in 2003 for its protection and promotion.

Nowadays

My passion to make the website

As a Chinese international student from a country with a history spanning five millennia, I have always been captivated by the rich tapestry of our country's cultural heritage. It is a legacy that has endured the test of time, passed down through centuries of change. Motivated by my love for our cultural treasures and a sense of urgency spurred by their gradual disappearance, I created a platform dedicated to promoting China's intangible cultural heritage. Immersing myself in folk literature, music and dance, traditional drama and storytelling, acrobatics and sports, fine arts, and traditional medicine, I've curated a comprehensive showcase of these cultural elements on Chinese Intangible Cultural Heritage Website. Whether it's delving into ancient folklore, experiencing the charm of traditional music, or witnessing the magic of Chinese theater, my aim is to educate, engage, and ignite a love for intangible cultural heritage. I work hard to promote China's excellent intangible cultural heritage on my website, hoping to attract more people's attention to this excellent culture and attract more people to understand, appreciate and inherit it. I believe that through collective efforts we can ensure that these valuable cultural traditions are protected and continue to grow. Join us in exploring the richness of China's intangible cultural heritage and in carrying forward our cultural legacy!
